I’m Claire. I am a dead feral cat. Currently I live in Chicago, where I grew up. It’s close to 2am on a Sunday as I’m composing this bio, which is why everything sounds so clumsy.

I grew up on the Internet, where I focus the majority of my attention and time. I’m deeply interested in the use of digital mediums to create meaning, in addition to the digital evolution which language is currently undergoing.

My primary goal in writing has always been to reveal the inherent comfort and cruelty of the reality hallucination.
