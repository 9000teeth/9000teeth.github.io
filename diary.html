19 jun 2021

June! Certainly, this is the only month that begins with new hope and ends not with longing but a kind of severance.

I have always felt most at peace in—most connected to—September: Wilt as presentiment, the blush of subtle gold not quite signifying what we both know will come. And November, too, its cold rain and sense of falsified nostalgia.

But what is June? I unclench my hand and find nothing there.
